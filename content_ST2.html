Representation in STEM

Women remain underrepresented in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields worldwide. Despite progress, many industries still have significantly fewer women than men.

Barriers Women Face

Women in STEM often face challenges such as gender bias, lack of role models, unequal opportunities, and workplace discrimination. These barriers can discourage women from entering or continuing in STEM careers.

Why It Matters

Increasing female participation in STEM leads to greater innovation, diversity of thought, and economic growth. Gender equality in these fields ensures that everyone has equal opportunities to succeed.

Solutions and Progress

Encouraging girls to pursue STEM education, promoting female role models, and creating inclusive workplaces are key steps toward equality. Many organisations are working to close the gender gap in STEM.
