Reflection

While developing my website, I worked on both the splash screen and the content page, which helped me improve different areas of my skills. Creating the splash screen was challenging at first, especially when trying to get the background image, fade-out effect, and timing of the redirect to work smoothly together. I had to troubleshoot issues like incorrect file paths and ensure the design looked visually appealing while still meeting the requirements. On the content page, I focused more on structure and organisation, ensuring proper headings, sections, and internal navigation so the information was easy to follow. Writing about women in STEM also required me to research and present the information clearly and in my own words. Overall, this project helped me develop both my technical skills, such as using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and my ability to design a user-friendly and well-structured website.
