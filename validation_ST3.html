Reflection on the Validation Process

During the validation process, I checked each of my pages using the W3C validator and corrected any issues before pasting the screenshot. This process helped me identify small mistakes such as missing separators in links, incorrect formatting, or structural issues that could affect page validity.

Validation was useful because it reminded me to pay attention to detail and make sure my pages followed proper HTML structure. It also helped me understand that even small code errors can affect how a page behaves or how reliably it is interpreted.

From this process, I learned the importance of testing pages carefully, checking links, and reviewing HTML structure before final submission. Validation helped improve the technical quality of my work and made my pages more reliable.
