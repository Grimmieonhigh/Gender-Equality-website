Meet the Team

Our team worked together to create a website focused on gender equality. Each member contributed different pages and features to support the overall design and purpose of the site.


Each team member has completed their own content page, page editor and validations page along with their main focus for the project.


As a team, we communicated regularly to ensure consistency in design, navigation, and overall user experience across all pages.
